My Approach

Professional Background

  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BScN): Built a strong foundation in human health, disabilities, diseases, and conditions through diverse nursing placements.
  • Mental Health Nursing Practicum: Found my true calling, deepening my fascination with mental health and working with individuals from diverse backgrounds facing both physical and mental health challenges.
  • Specialization in Perinatal and Postpartum Mental Health: My passion lies in supporting individuals through life's most transformative and challenging moments, blending my nursing expertise with a deep commitment to providing emotional support during significant transitions.
  • Master of Arts in Counselling Psychology (MACP): Deepened my knowledge and honed my skills in psychotherapy, collaborating with like-minded peers.
  • Practicum at Roots In Wellness: Gained valuable hands-on counselling experience while continuing to provide compassionate support to clients.

A Little About Me

I was born in Quebec and had the privilege of attending school in English, which has made me fluently bilingual in both French and English. I now live in a peaceful town just outside of Ottawa with my husband, son, and our three beloved cats. Staying active and connected to nature is important to me—whether it's biking and walking along the scenic Mississippi River in the warmer months or swimming indoors and skating during the winter. I also enjoy engaging in creative projects, which provide a different kind of balance and fulfillment. These activities help me maintain overall well-being, something I deeply value in both my personal and professional life.

Having personally experienced counselling, I deeply value the insight and growth that therapy can bring. My own journey as a client has shaped the way I approach my work, reinforcing my belief that healing happens in a supportive, non-judgmental space. Helping others is my passion, and I would be privileged to walk alongside you on your path to healing and self-discovery.
